What is energy philanthropy?

Energy Philanthropy is about freely giving your resources, skills, and time without expecting anything in return. It's not the typical "how much are you going to give me" before I do something for you attitude.

In practice, it can take many forms, from volunteering to work on a project, donating money to support a cause, or even offering encouragement and support to someone in need.

Simply put, it's about giving your time and energy to someone without an ego attitude. Such as, "How much money do I get for doing this?"

I practice Energy Philanthropy. This means I will give freely to you and your project without expectations or conditions. We'll collaborate together as friends to complete your project.
